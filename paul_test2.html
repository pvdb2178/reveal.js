Key take-aways

Technology must have low adoption barrier
  1. Tools needs to be scalable (Lambda)
  2. Ensure solutions are accesible, e.g. publish on Github
  3. Facilitate easy replication by using open source solutions & permissive licensing
Organisational conext is important
  1. Framing can make a big difference
  2. Need to have the right people in the room
  3. Understand how the data is generated
Work on high impact problems
  1. Problems need to be common and re-use proven solutions where possible
  2. Need to have a deep undersanding of what the 'business' problem is
  3. Collaborate with
